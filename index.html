Bio

I was raised in Boise Idaho. I grew up loving the outdoors. I was always on the move. When I was 19, after my first year of college at Boise State University, I moved to Pocatello to attend school at Idaho State University where I received a Bachelors of Business Administration in Marketing. After college I worked for 2 years in Pocatello until I decided I needed something more in my life. This is when I moved to New York City. I moved here for work and most importantly to live closer to my partner in crime, Sidrah. I lived there for a year and a half working mainly at the United Nations Federal Credit Union and part-time at Hell's Kitchen restaurant in the Hell's Kitchen area. After working at a Credit Union for the past 3 years I decided that I want to do something more with my life and my career so I decided to pursue a career in Computer Programming. This is when I found BoiseCodeWorks Full-Stack Development course. I recently finished the course and I am currently working with other developers and entreprenuers to create applications which they, and myself, believe will provide consumers with a service that will solve problems in various markets.
